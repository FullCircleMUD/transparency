## Summary

FullCircleMUD is a text-based multiplayer online game
whose in-game economy operates on the XRP Ledger.
Gold, resources, and items exist as XRPL tokens,
giving players true ownership of their game assets.

**FullCircleMUD does not offer gold redemption.** There
is no mechanism to exchange gold for RLUSD, fiat
currency, or any other asset through FullCircleMUD.
In-game gold is a game item, not a financial product.

Players may trade game tokens on the XRPL's
protocol-native decentralised exchange (DEX) and
automated market maker (AMM) at their own initiative
and risk. FullCircleMUD does not operate or control
the XRPL DEX or AMM.

The full compliance framework will be published here
before mainnet launch.
