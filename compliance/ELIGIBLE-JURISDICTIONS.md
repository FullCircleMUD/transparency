# FullCircleMUD — Eligible Jurisdictions

*Last updated: March 2026*
*Next scheduled review: September 2026*

---

## Overview

The gold redemption feature is available to players
accessing FullCircleMUD from the following countries,
subject to account login history being consistent with
access from an eligible jurisdiction.

Eligibility is determined by physical location at the
time of access, not nationality or permanent residence.
A player physically located in an eligible country at
the time of a redemption request is eligible regardless
of their country of citizenship or ordinary residence.

This list is reviewed every six months and updated
when material regulatory changes occur in any
jurisdiction. Changes are recorded in the monthly
transparency report for the period in which they occur.

For the full compliance framework governing eligibility
determination, see
[COMPLIANCE-FRAMEWORK.md](COMPLIANCE-FRAMEWORK.md).

## Not Listed

If your country is not listed, the gold redemption
feature is not currently available in your region.

This may be because:

- Your jurisdiction has financial regulations that
  would require FullCircleMUD to obtain licences or
  registrations not currently held
- Your jurisdiction has not yet been assessed
- Your jurisdiction is under review

The absence of a country from this list does not
reflect any judgement about that country. It reflects
FullCircleMUD's current regulatory position.

Players in unlisted jurisdictions may still play
FullCircleMUD and participate fully in the game
economy. Gold and items can be earned, held, and
traded freely. The redemption feature — direct
exchange of gold for RLUSD through the FullCircleMUD
website — is the only feature affected by
jurisdictional eligibility.

## Restricted Jurisdictions

The following jurisdictions are explicitly restricted.
The gold redemption feature is not available to players
accessing FullCircleMUD from these locations.

- United States of America (including all territories)
- All European Economic Area member states
- United Kingdom
- Switzerland
- Canada
- Japan
- South Korea

*Additional jurisdictions may be restricted where
applicable financial regulations require it.*
